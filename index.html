About Us

At Sì-tudio by Sistubyssie, we bring Vietnamese artistry and passion to every look we create. Founded with a love for hair, makeup, and bridal beauty, our studio offers a luxurious, welcoming space where you can feel confident and glamorous.

Originally from Vietnam 🇻🇳 and soon expanding to Australia 🇦🇺, we are dedicated to using premium products and expert techniques to make you look and feel your absolute best — whether it's your wedding day, a special event, or everyday glam.
